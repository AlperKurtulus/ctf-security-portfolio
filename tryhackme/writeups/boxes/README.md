## üí° Tips for Success

1. **Enumerate Thoroughly**
   - Spend time on enumeration
   - Check all services and ports
   - Don't rush to exploitation

2. **Document Everything**
   - Take screenshots
   - Save command output
   - Track your methodology

3. **Try Harder‚Ñ¢**
   - Don't give up easily
   - Research unknown services
   - Think outside the box

4. **Use Automation Wisely**

   - Manual verification is crucial
   - Understand what tools do

5. **Learn from Failures**
   - Failed attempts teach lessons
   - Review what didn't work
   - Adjust your approach
